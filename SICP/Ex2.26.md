```scheme
(define x (list 1 2 3))
(define y (list 4 5 6))
```
- `(append x y)` will be printed as `(1 2 3 4 5 6)`

- `(cons x y)` will be printed as `((1 2 3) 4 5 6)`

- `(list x y)` will be printed as `((1 2 3) (4 5 6))`
